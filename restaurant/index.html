About Rigoni

Rigoni is an Italian restaurant located in the heart of Buenos Aires. Since its opening in 1998, it offers authentic food based on traditional Italian cuisine. Rigoni is characterized by her concern for people with some type of food intolerance. The special menus suitable for celiacs, lactose intolerant or people who are following some type of diet stand out. A table full of traditional products that you can accompany thanks to an extensive wine list that includes specimens of Italian and Spanish origin.
